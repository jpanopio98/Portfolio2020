Mountainscapes

Mountainscapes is another project I made from Adobe Illustrator. At this point in time, I am quite used to the Adobe Illustrator software and the pen tool, specifically. I wanted to create these shapes to represent mountain ridges and hills. I played around with the colors to represent perspective with the darker foreground being the nearest to the viewer. I created Mountainscapes as inspiration from my favorite artist, Katsushika Hokusai, who did woodblock prints on landscapes.
