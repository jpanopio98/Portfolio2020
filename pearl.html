Pearl of the Orient

Pearl of the Orient is a collection of photographs from my trip to the Philippines. These photos were shot in Manila and Pampanga, Philippines. This collection of photographs are meant to serve as a memory of my trip to the Philippines. It was a surreal experience to take pictures in the city and capture the natural beauty of the countryside.

Jedd Panopio | PORTFOLIO 2020
