Escape

Escape is my first capstone project during my Senior year at San Jose State University. I wanted to create a futuristic,alien-like landscape as a means of escape from quarantine and the problems of the world. My goal for this project was to have the viewer be immersed in the environment. I used Blender as my means of creating this landscape.
