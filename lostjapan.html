Lost In Japan

Lost in Japan is a collection of photographs from my trip to Japan. I wanted to capture the essence of everyday Japanese life through these photos. I also wanted these photos to serve as my memories from Japan and the places I visited. I wanted to use a dark lighting to give off a gloomy vibe to create an aesthetic.
