Cityscape

Cityscapes is my second series of projects using Adobe Illustrator. When I started these, I was already starting to get used to the Illustrator program. For this piece, I studied and played around with background,middleground and foreground. I wanted to use different views of cityscapes to show the illusion of perspective in a 2D space. I also played around with different color palettes to represent different times of the day.

Jedd Panopio | PORTFOLIO 2020
