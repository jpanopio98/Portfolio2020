Rescue Eva

Using the Godot engine,This game is about a funky dancing robot. He is on a journey to find out where his "girlfriend" is. He comes across lots of trouble as he tries to break through to reach his "girlfriend". Future progress on this game is unknown. Game was developed by Welby, Linhson, Jed, and Alison
