Fate

Fate is based of off the animated movie "Your Name" by Makoto Shinkai. For this piece, i was inspired by the beautiful animated scenes from "Your Name" and wanted to create a complex vector piece in Illustrator. I wanted to work with complex shapes and use tools like the brush tool and the gradient effect. I learned a lot about working with shadows and silhouettes in this piece as that was the most challenging part of the process.
