Anime in Real Life

Using Adobe Photoshop, I wanted to combine these fictional anime characters to real life locations as if they were real people. With this piece, I wanted to convey the connection us humans with virtual characters in our everyday lives, with artificial intelligence being a prime example. The Japanese text can be translated into human emotions such as love and loneliness.

Jedd Panopio | PORTFOLIO 2020
