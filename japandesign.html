Japan Postcard Designs

Using Photoshop, I took a couple of stock images from the internet and one of my own to create these Japanese Postcards for my Design 83 class at San Jose State University. My intention for creating this series of postcards was to make use of shapes and text design in Photoshop. The Japanese text translates to the location of the particular picture. I wanted to use the white and red colors to reflect Japan's colors.
