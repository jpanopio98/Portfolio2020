To Get to You

To Get to to You is a personal project that explores the idea of escapism and the concept of running away from one's problems. I wanted to explore the idea of escapism through my knowledge of Photoshop and photoediting by taking stock images and combining them to create a narrative. I think the idea of escapism is very relevant today mainly because of the current pandemic and the need to unwind and entertain one's self during the quarantine.
