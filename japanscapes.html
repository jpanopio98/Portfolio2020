Japanscapes

Japanscape is my first series of projects using Illustrator. I wanted to learn Adobe Illustrator to broaden my skill set during quarantine. For this series I wanted to use shapes to create minimal and simple landscapes and play around with several color palettes. I created this project to learn the basics of vector art and broaden my knowledge of design and composition.
